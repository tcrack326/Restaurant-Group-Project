Founded by madam Wendy, monsieurs Paul and Tom, and le Chef Griffeth, Le Cochon de Fer (the Iron Pig) was opened in 2010.

The idea came about when Monsieru Paul, from Big Major Cay island, was becoming over-populated with his sounder of swine that he decided the best way to control his pig population was to make his overabundance available to the people of the mainland. With the help of his good friends and now business partners Wendy, Tom, and chef Griffeth, they created a seasonally exquisite menu filled with fresh, locally sourced ingredients, spices and of course, pork. The result is food that is honest, straightforward and pays homage to the bounty of Major Cay Island.

The restaurant was a huge hit from the beginning, with eager mainlanders sometimes waiting hours for an opportunity to experience le Cochon de Fer with its warm hues, soft lighting and relaxed ambience that invited guests to settle in, get comfortable, and have a great time. Located on the main island of Fowl Cay and within a minutes walk from the island's major resort, le Cochon de Fer is the highest class fine dining any one can experience within the Bahamas.

The le Cochon de Fer experience is about more than just an amazing menu. We set out to offer our guests a getaway experience they cannot find anywhere else.
